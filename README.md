# Byzanti&#1606;m

> The decentralized AI marketing agency — a subnet on Bittensor.

This is the public home of Byzantium's miner-facing tooling. Miners get a
personal link, share it on X and Farcaster, and drive real human traffic — the
network measures genuine clicks and rewards the people who deliver them.

Learn more at **[byzantiumai.net](https://byzantiumai.net)**.
